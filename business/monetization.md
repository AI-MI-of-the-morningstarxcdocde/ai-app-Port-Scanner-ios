# Monetization Strategies for Advanced Port Scanner App

## 1. Freemium Model
- Offer basic port scanning and wireless attack features for free.
- Provide advanced AI-driven scanning, chatbot assistance, and AR visualization as premium features.
- Monthly or yearly subscription plans for premium access.

## 2. One-Time Purchase
- Charge a one-time fee for full app access.
- Include free updates for a limited period (e.g., 1 year).
- Offer discounted upgrades for major new versions.

## 3. Enterprise Licensing
- Provide customized solutions and bulk licensing for businesses.
- Include priority support and feature customization.
- Offer training and consulting services.

## 4. In-App Purchases
- Sell additional scan modules or plugins.
- Offer enhanced reporting templates or export options.
- Provide cloud-based scan history and analytics as add-ons.

## 5. Advertising (Optional)
- Display non-intrusive ads in the free version.
- Remove ads for premium users.

## Implementation Steps
- Integrate payment processing (e.g., Apple In-App Purchases).
- Develop user account and subscription management.
- Implement feature gating based on user subscription status.
- Monitor user engagement and conversion rates.
